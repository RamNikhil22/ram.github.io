Andhra Pradesh is located in the south-eastern India and is part of the linguistic-cultural region of South India. It is surrounded by Maharashtra, Chhattisgarh and Orissa in the north, the Bay of Bengal in the East, Tamil Nadu to the south and Karnataka to the west. Hyderabad is capital city Andhrapradesh.
