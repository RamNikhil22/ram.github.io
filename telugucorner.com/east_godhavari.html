East Godhavari

East Godhavari (Thoorpu Godhavari) is district in Andhrapradesh. East Godhavari is a mostly agricultural district. In this district, there are famous places and good temples. There are so many pilgrims in East godhavari. Here are few important pilgrimages in East godhavari.
